Key Features

Safe, accurate, and customizable local OCR

🔒

Fully Offline

All processing happens in your browser. No images or text are ever sent to external servers.

🏛️

NDL OCR Technology

Powered by NDLOCR-Lite from the National Diet Library of Japan. High-accuracy Japanese text recognition.

📐

Vertical & Horizontal

Accurately recognizes both horizontal and vertical Japanese text, including old books and legal documents.

🚀

3 Ways to Launch

Toolbar icon, keyboard shortcut (Alt+Shift+O), or right-click context menu.

🧹

Regex Cleaning Rules

Auto-clean OCR results before copying — remove commas, spaces, line breaks with custom regex rules.

🌐

Multilingual UI

Automatically switches between Japanese and English based on your browser language settings.
